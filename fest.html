Social Fest

Aim: We live in a fast-moving world that has several social issues on one side and innovations that help our society on the other, both evolving every day. The aim of having a socio-weekend is to spread awareness of current social issues, which requires concern and action from society and depicts the utilization of innovations in our society to the youth of our country. As society comprises a wide variety of individuals representing different professions, communities, opinions, a socio-weekend encompasses interaction with experts and personalities of various fields. The socio-weekend is a platform for multiple issues to be discussed, depicted, and performed in various forms of events so that one gets to observe society from different viewpoints.

Health Checkup Camp

Aim: In this fast-moving world, people tend to disregard their health and sometimes even health problems they might be unaware of. Regular health check-ups can help find potential health issues before they become a problem as it is rightly said - ‘Prevention is better than cure. The camp covered all basic checks ups including Anthropometry, Blood Pressure, Blood Sugar Level, Dental and General examinations.

Impact: People can get aware if they have any health issues and hence can take proper treatment for the same. 244 people participated in the health checkup camp.


Turn Setbacks into Comebacks

Aim: Quitting any kind of addiction is very difficult but can be treated if provided with proper treatment and motivation. Through this event, we aim to educate people on different types of addictions(alcohol, drugs, social media, etc.) and identify if they are going through one of them.

Impact: Participants can get to learn how to tackle any kind of addiction they might be facing.

Author Talk

Aim: Online classes due to the pandemic have led to isolation and unhealthy attachment to social media among many. This has led to a reduced attention span among students. In the minds of the youth who are the future changemakers, we aim to reinforce the importance of positive and diverse relationships with empathy, reflection, perspective, an open mind, and respect through inculcating a reading habit among the youth.

Impact: Participants can learn the importance and benefits of regular reading.
